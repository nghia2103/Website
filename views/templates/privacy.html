Accessibility Statement

At DONKIES, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

1. Accessibility Features

Our website has been designed to be accessible and user-friendly for everyone. Some of the features include:

  • Text resizing options
  • High-contrast color scheme for better visibility
  • Keyboard navigation for easier browsing
  • Alternative text for images

2. Compatibility

Our website is compatible with modern browsers and screen readers. We strive to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 standards to ensure accessibility for people with disabilities.

3. Feedback

If you encounter any issues or need assistance with accessibility features on our site, please contact us at ABC@GMAIL.COM. We are happy to help and will work to resolve any issues as quickly as possible.

4. Ongoing Commitment

We continue to monitor and improve the accessibility of our website to provide the best experience for all users. If you have suggestions for improving accessibility, please let us know.
